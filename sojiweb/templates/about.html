Jake Rose

Chief Design Officer

Jake, a junior at the North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics, is the Chief Design Officer in Soji, developing the Remora for production. He has extensive experience with 3-D computer-aided design and manufacturing software such as Fusion 360 and Meshmixer, and with manufacturing techniques such as 3-D printing and CNC subtractive manufacturing. Jake has also competed in multiple STEM competitions such as Seaperch, an underwater robotics competition. Out of four years competing, he has qualified for the international competition three years in a row. The last two years he has designed and 3-D printed his robots from scratch. Jake also runs a separate online business selling 3-D printed customizable maps.

Evan Zhang

Chief Technology Officer

Evan, a senior at the North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics, is an aspiring computer programmer who has created various websites and software programs in Python, Javascript, and markup languages. Evan is also an aspiring entrepreneur, recently founding SMath Tutors, a non-profit tutoring organization, working as an intern at KickIt, a small social media app, and reaching the semifinals in the 2020 Quarter Zero competition.
