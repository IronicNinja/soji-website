Remora is an unconventional and innovative shower caddy that brings both convenience and utility. When using it, simply unfold at the hinge and connect the 2 suction cups onto the bathroom or shower wall for easy access to personal items. This keeps the shower caddy from collecting water and helps avoid direct contact with the unsanitary bathroom floor. Due to its compact size, Remora makes an ideal companion for traveling, not just showering, and can be utilized for a variety of situations and locations. Dividers can be provided to allow the consumer to sectionalize their caddy in a customizable manner.
